How to Use Fragrance

EASY USE WITH 4 STEPS
  • 1
    Shake the perfume bottle gently before each use to ensure proper mixing of the fragrance.
  • 2
    On clean skin, spray once or twice on desired areas, preferably pulse points.
  • 3
    Allow the perfume to dry naturally without rubbing on the skin.
  • 4
    For a longer-lasting scent, consider layering with scented body lotions or creams.
